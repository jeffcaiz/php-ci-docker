# php-ci-docker
A docker image mainly focus on CI of PHP, especially for Laravel

Currenly only 7.3 is available.

#### Extension installed:

1. zip
1. curl
1. iconv
1. mbstring
1. pdo
1. pdo_mysql
1. pdo_pgsql
1. pdo_sqlite
1. pcntl
1. tokenizer
1. xml
1. gd
1. bcmath
1. intl
1. xdebug

#### Tools/binutils installed:

1. composer
1. phpcs

It's based on the great work of https://github.com/lorisleiva/laravel-docker
